# Privacy Policy for GoodRunss

**Last Updated: December 6, 2024**

## Introduction

GoodRunss ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card details)
- **Location Data**: Real-time and historical location to show nearby venues and trainers
- **Health Data**: Workout data from Apple Health and Strava (with your permission)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration, crash reports
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates when using location-based features

### User-Generated Content
- **Reviews and Ratings**: Venue and trainer reviews you submit
- **Messages**: Communications with trainers and other users
- **Photos**: Profile pictures and venue photos you upload
- **Check-ins**: Venue check-in data and activity logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services**: Connect you with trainers, venues, and other users
- **Process Payments**: Handle bookings and subscription payments via Stripe
- **Personalization**: Recommend trainers, venues, and activities based on your preferences
- **AI Assistant (GIA)**: Provide personalized fitness recommendations and answers
- **Location Services**: Show nearby venues, courts, and trainers
- **Health Integration**: Sync workout data with Apple Health and Strava
- **Communication**: Send booking confirmations, notifications, and updates
- **Analytics**: Improve app performance and user experience
- **Safety**: Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Your Information With:

**Service Providers**:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **Firebase/Google Cloud**: Backend infrastructure and database
- **OpenAI**: AI-powered features (GIA assistant)
- **Google Places API**: Venue information
- **Apple Health**: Health data sync (with your permission)
- **Strava**: Activity data sync (with your permission)

**Other Users**:
- Profile information (name, photo, ratings) visible to other users
- Reviews and ratings are public
- Check-ins may be visible to other users at the same venue

**Legal Requirements**:
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your health data without explicit permission
- Use your location data when the app is not in use (unless you enable background location)

## Your Privacy Rights

### You Have the Right To:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable location services, health data sync, or notifications
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in a common format

### To Exercise Your Rights:
Contact us at privacy@goodrunss.com

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure login with Firebase Authentication
- **Payment Security**: PCI-compliant payment processing via Stripe
- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access to personal data

## Children's Privacy

GoodRunss is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Location Data

### How We Use Location:
- Show nearby venues, courts, and trainers
- Provide directions to venues
- Display activity heat maps
- Personalize recommendations

### Your Control:
- You can disable location services in device settings
- We only access location when the app is in use (foreground)
- You can delete location history at any time

## Health Data

### Apple Health Integration:
- **Data We Read**: Workout sessions, active calories, distance
- **Data We Write**: Workout sessions you complete via GoodRunss
- **Your Control**: Manage permissions in Apple Health settings
- **Privacy**: Health data is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud (if enabled)

### Strava Integration:
- **Data We Access**: Activities, stats, achievements
- **Your Control**: Revoke access anytime in Strava settings
- **Privacy**: We only access data you explicitly authorize

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.
